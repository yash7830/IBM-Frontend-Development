Education Breakthrough

After Receiving a scholarship, Raj, a former street child in India, graduated from college and now works as a teacher, helping others escape poverty.

Women's Empowerment

A Women's Empowerment cooperative in UP started a successful textile business, providing economic independence and leadership opportunities for its members.

Blood Donation, Food Serving, and Health

We started Swami Vivekananda Free Evening School with the help of one of our colleague's parents in Patan, Gujarat. From 2018 to 2022, we had 200 poor children to whom volunteers, college students, retired professors/teachers taught in the evening free of cost. Many donors contributed money, clothes, food, exam kits, notebooks, etc. We also successfully arranged blood donation camps, food distributions, and free health checkups.
